Hamburger

Hamburgers originated in Hamburg, Germany in 1840 by August Cheesewiz. These tasty meat and bun combos only need these ingredients:

  • Ground meat
  • Whole wheat buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Mayo

Take all these ingredient and let's get cooking! Yum yum!

  1. Form meat into patties.
  2. Brown patties in skillet
  3. Toast buns in toaster.
