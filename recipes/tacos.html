Beef Tacos

Beef tacos are loved by everyone far and wide and are a delicacy across the world. This recipe is easy to make and you only need these 5 ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • Sour cream

    • Now let's get cooking!

    1. Brown ground beef in a hot pan.
    2. Drain grease and set aside.
    3. Crisp up tortilla in the pan.
    4. Add lettuce, cheese and sour cream.
